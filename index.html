Mandela Effect Examples

  • Nelson Mandela's Death: Many people remember Nelson Mandela dying in prison in the 1980s, but he actually passed away in 2013.
  • Berenstain Bears: Some remember the children's book series as "Berenstein Bears," but it has always been spelled "Berenstain Bears."
  • Monopoly Man's Monocle: Many recall the Monopoly mascot having a monocle, but he never did.
  • Oscar Mayer: Some remember the brand name as "Oscar Meyer," but it's actually "Oscar Mayer."
  • Fruit of the Loom Logo: People recall a cornucopia in the logo, but it has never been there.
  • Febreze: Many remember the air freshener brand spelled as "Febreeze," but it's "Febreze."
  • Looney Tunes: Some remember it as "Looney Toons," but it's actually "Looney Tunes."
  • KitKat: Many recall a dash in the name (Kit-Kat), but there has never been one.
  • Curious George's Tail: People remember the character having a tail, but he does not.
  • We Are the Champions: Some remember the song ending with "of the world," but that's not part of the studio version.
